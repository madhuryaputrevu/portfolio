EGGY .

The cute, interactive toy fostering emotional understanding and expression.

With four detachable yolk parts that personify basic emotions - Happiness, Anger, Sadness, Surprise - Eggy is a valuable developmental toy for kids between aged between 3-7 years old.
By changing the yolk part based on their current emotion, children can learn to recognize and articulate their feelings, fostering emotional intelligence and communication skills. Its a creative way to engage children in emotional learning and development.
